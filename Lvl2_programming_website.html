Who he is

William Henry Gates III was born on October the 28th, 1955, and is famous for being the co-founder of Microsoft, a leader in software and technology development. Microsoft have developed various Personal Computers and Operating system, as well as many other things to do with the PC industry, such as software. Microsoft also creates game consoles such as the Xbox. Bill and his wife Melinda also founded the Gates Foundation. This foundation donates to various charities, causes and people in need.
